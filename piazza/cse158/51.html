We’re EDGE, a free, year-long enrichment program offered to help introduce STEM-related fields to high school girls hosted by UCSD’s Society of Women Engineers and Women In Computing. 

\n

\n

We’re reaching out to ask if you can advertise our mentor applications to your class. Mentors will be a resource to the high school students for the duration of the 2024-2025 school year and are expected to attend 1-2 events per quarter. It is a low commitment, but can make a great impact on our community!
